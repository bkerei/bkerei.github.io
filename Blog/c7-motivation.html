Search within yourself by Chade-Meng Tan, Chapter 6: Making Profits, Rowing Across Oceans, and Changing the World

How does life look through the 'pleasure, passion and purpose' lens?

To be fair, right now, not great. My passion gets me into trouble and I have no pleasure in my career. I have just finished a long planning obligation and I'm just tired to my core. Outside of this my purpose is to get to BootCamp and be in the thick of becoming who I want to be moving forward. My passion and pleasure and purpose feel like they are on hold.

From the 'discovering values and higher purpose' exercise:

What are your core values?

I did this exercise funnily enough this time last year. At the time it was too be supportive of myself and others, to be kind and to be open to change. Revisting this I found that I felt the same but also more optimistic. My core values are"

To be kind and practice kindness.

To be open to changes, different ideals and different opinions.

To share knowledge with others to help for the greater good.

To leave a legacy of kindeness and warmth after I've gone.

What do you stand for?

Kindness. To be kind to others and myself and make improvements that make technology/life kinder.

How did you find the resilience ideas and meditation exercise?

Excellent! I'm going through something unpleasant at the moment and the resilence exercises have helped with the rage breathing that satrts and helps me get my head in order so I can speak. Meditation has been my saving grace and its been super helpful

Any other musings?

I do feel more motivated to get things done, thank you for this chapter!
