Search within yourself by Chade-Meng Tan, Chapter 3: Mindfulness without the butt on cushion

What did you learn from your 'mindfulness conversation'? How did the other person find it?

I use this technique with the members that call me at work, but I've never asked for feedback. I spent a break talking to my friend Cam. I told him what i was doing and I stood and listened to him and then asked question till I got to the point of his story. The feedback I got was that I did agood job and I was very calming. I did counceling at Waikato Uni and the active listening stategy is the same and I got a B+ on my assignment for this, and again I do this for a job so I'm well versed in this technique.

Which of the three suggested techniques will you use to sustain your practice?

I've incoperated the "Be nice to yourself" and "Send the distractions away with kindness". I'm not keen on the eye open technique, I like closing my eye when I'm meditating, just makes me feel calmer and more in the right mindset to not think. If that makes sense...

Any other musings?

Meditating Monday to Friday and Mindfully walking on the weekend has been been life changing. If I don't meditate I can feel it by the way things hit me emotionally during the day. I am calmer. kinder and just easier going and I love it! I also think I look younger.
